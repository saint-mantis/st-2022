DETAILS ABOUT SSC [STAFF SELECTION COMMISSION]



• SSC conducts the exams to fill the vacancies in the Central Government Service. For different vacancies they ask different qualification like degree, +2, SSLC etc.,

• The examinations for which degree is the basic qualification (CGL) are conducted at 4 stages – TIER 1, TIER 2, TIER 3 and TIER 4.

• The examinations for which +2 is the basic qualification (CHSL) are conducted at 3 stages – TIER 1, TIER 2 and TIER 3.

• The examinations for which 10 TH is the basic qualification (MTS) are conducted at 2 stages – TIER 1 and TIER 2
