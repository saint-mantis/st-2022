SSC CGL [Combined Graduate Level]

Basic Qualification for SSC CGL: Degree This exam is conducted in 4 phases as below


Subjects

    • Intelligence and Reasoning (25 questions)

    • General Awareness (25 questions)

    • Quantitative Aptitude (25 questions)

    • English Comprehensive (25 questions)

    Each question carries 2 marks and there is a ¼ negative marking associated with every wrong answer.

    After completing tier 1, the students should attend tier 2.


    The followings are subjects of Tier 2(Online)


    Subjects


    • Quantitative Abilities (100 questions)

    • English Language (100 questions)

    • Statistics (100 questions)

    • General Studies (100 questions)

    Each question carriers 2 marks and there is a ¼ negative marking associated with every wrong answer, excepting in the case of English language section, where each question carries 1 mark along with ¼ negative marking.

    After clearing tier-2, the student should attend tier 3.
    The following are the subjects of Tier -3 (Descriptive)

    Subjects


    • English or Hindi (optional)

    • Essay Writing

    • Precise writing

    • Letter Writing

    • Application Writing etc.

    After clearing tier-3, the students should attend tier -4 The followings are the subjects of Tier -4 (Descriptive)

    Subjects


    • Computer Proficiency Test

    • Data Entry Skill Test
