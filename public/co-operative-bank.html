Co-operative Banks

The Co-operative Service Examination Board (CSEB) conducts the examinations for the vacancies arising in different co-operative societies such as junior clerk, secretary etc.


Qualification


    • B com with co-operation

    • B.com without co-operation as there elective subject and BA, BSC Students should pass HDC (Higher Diploma in Co-Operation)

    • 10 th + JDC (Junior Diploma in Co-Operation)


    Subjects for the Exam


    • General English (10 marks)

    • General knowledge (10 marks)

    • Test of reasoning (10 marks)

    • Co-optative act and rules,


    • Accounting, banking, co-operative, Principles and practices (50marks)

    The exam will consist of 160 questions with .5 marks each for correct answers and no negative marking
